Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Block Dash is a mobile game created for entertainment. This Privacy Policy explains what information the game uses and how it is handled.

Information We Store

Block Dash stores game settings, vibration and sound preferences, and best score data locally on your device. This information is used only to make the game work properly and remember your preferences.

Personal Information

Block Dash does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, or other personal information inside the game.

Analytics, Ads, and Purchases

The current version of Block Dash does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or in-app purchases. If these features are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before release.

Platform Services

If you download Block Dash through an app store, Apple, Google, or the relevant platform provider may collect information according to their own privacy policies and developer tools.

Children's Privacy

Block Dash is a general-audience game. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Because Block Dash stores game preferences and best score locally, you can delete this local data by uninstalling the app from your device.
